About a Legacy

Allow me to introduce you to the Mississippi Pizza family! My name is Ashley and I am the owner of the company. Born and raised in Mississippi, Ashley always wanted to start a business and also pour positivity into the community around her. In 2007, she got her first job in a pizza place and within a year and a half was promoted to store manager. In 2009, the idea of owning a pizza company started to beome not only a dream, but a reality. In 2010, Mississippi Pizza was born and has been growing ever since. Mississippi Pizza has been on a mission breathing life into the community with such things like hosting parties, having and promoting local talent. We believe in supporting our local community and have been involved in various community events and fundraisers. The Mississippi Pizza has one 1st and 2nd place in our pizza festival. Alongside obtaining 2nd place for our famous Ragin Cajun Pizza in the Specialty catergory in the State Pizza Contest. Our goal is to create a welcoming environment for everyone. Mississippi Pizza is a pizza restaurant located in West Point, Mississippi. We have been serving up our community for 10 beautiful years. We are known for our live music and family-friendly atmosphere like: karaoke, boardgame night, and open mic night. Mississippi Pizza has been a proud steeple in the community where kids can enjoy themselves and adults have a place to enjoy and have good clean fun.
